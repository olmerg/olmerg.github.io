Interactive Boards: Machine Learning Educational Tools

Designed for Business Impact: These interactive dashboards help data scientists, analysts, and decision-makers understand ML model evaluation and performance trade-offs from a business perspective.

  • Evaluación de Modelos de Machine Learning: Valor de NegocioExplore how different classification metrics impact business outcomes. Understand the trade-offs between precision, recall, and F1-score in real scenarios.
  • Impacto de Errores de Regresión en Bienes RaícesVisualize how regression errors translate to real financial impact. See MAE, RMSE, and MAPE with business context.
  • Comparando Regresores: Lasso, Ridge y RANSACInteractive comparison of regression techniques. Understand regularization and robustness through visual experimentation.
  • Evaluación de Clasificadores en Triage de UrgenciasClassifier evaluation in a healthcare context: medical triage classification.
  • Ejercicio: Distancias y OCR (pedagógico)Distance metrics and Optical Character Recognition learning tool.
  • Tablero Interactivo de Clustering K-MeansExplore clustering algorithms interactively.
  • Ejercicio MLP: Validacion de calculo manualStep-by-step MLP forward-pass practice with automatic validation of incorrect calculations.
  • Tablero Interactivo de Atención (Self-Attention)Understand attention mechanisms in transformer architectures.
